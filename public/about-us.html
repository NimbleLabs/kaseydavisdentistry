Meet Dr. Kasey Davis

Your Smiles Best Friend

Dr. Kasey Davis brings a lifelong passion for helping others to her practice. Raised in a small town near Birmingham, Dr. Davis graduated summa cum laude from the University of Montevallo with a Bachelor of Science in Biology. She earned her Doctorate of Dental Medicine from the UAB School of Dentistry, where she was honored with several prestigious scholarships and inducted into Omicron Kappa Upsilon, the National Dental Honor Society.


Outside of dentistry, Dr. Davis cherishes life with her husband, BJ and their five children. She absolutely loves to travel, always seeking new adventures and experiences with her family. When she's not exploring new destinations, youll find her listening to audible books or podcasts, having coffee with girlfriends, or simply soaking up the little moments that make life beautiful. Whether shes crafting confident smiles and faces in the office or enjoying a peaceful day at the beach, Dr. Davis lives by the same philosophy: care deeply, work passionately, and bring joy to every moment.  

Combining expertise and heart to deliver exceptional dental care.

Kourtney Office Manager/Insurance Coordinator

Since 2016, Kourtney has been dedicated to the dental field, specializing in insurance administration and patient care. Outside of work, she loves spending time with her husband and their son, as well as family and friends. Whether attending concerts or enjoying outside, Kourtney values creating meaningful memories with her loved ones.

MaryBeth Dental Assistant

MaryBeth began her career in dentistry in 2007. She is passionate about helping patients understand their treatment and complete necessary procedures with confidence. She enjoys creating a positive and supportive experience for every patient. In her free time, she loves spending time with her husband, Justin, and their children

Misty Hygienist

With 31 years of experience in the dental field, Misty is passionate about educating patients on the importance of oral health and helping them feel confident in their dental care. She takes the time to explain proper oral hygiene practices, and the connection between oral health and overall well-being. Misty resides in Lincoln, AL with her husband Randy, and two children, Carson and Anna Rose. In her free time, she enjoys planting flowers and working in the yard. 



Brandi Hygienist

With over 17 years of experience in dentistry, Brandi is a passionate and knowledgeable dental hygienist who loves helping patients feel confident about their oral health. She received her education through the UAB ADHP program and believes that education and prevention are the foundation of a healthy smile, and strives to make every patient feel comfortable and cared for. Outside of the office shes married to her best friend and expecting a baby girl. A hair and makeup artist in her free time. She also enjoys traveling to new places, catching live concerts, health and wellness, and making the most of lifes adventures.
